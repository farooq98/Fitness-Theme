Welcome To Prime Gymnastics Club!

Prime Gymnastics Club was established in 1987 as a non-profit organization to provide outstanding gymnastics training to people of all ages and abilities. Over the years, we have helped to inspire children of all abilities to pursue their dreams of becoming great gymnasts.

The club is run by an elected management committee, consisting of parent volunteers, full and part time administrative staff and an amazing team of highly qualified and experienced coaches. Our three-storey club facilities are equipped to allow us to offer a range of exciting recreational classes for toddlers, kids, teens, parents and adults. We run successful Women’s Artistic Gymnastics (WAG) and Men’s Artistic Gymnastics (MAG) competitive programmes.

Our Club’s vision is simply to be the best gymnastics club in the region through our mission and belief that every one of our gymnasts deserves the opportunity to reach their full potential.



Prime Gymnastics Club is one of very few clubs in Singapore which has the ability to develop gymnasts from beginners to elite competitive levels in the sport. We are proud of our gymnasts who have been selected to join the Singapore Gymnastics National Team and who have represented Singapore at major international competitions at the South East Asian Games (SEA Games), Asian Junior Championships, Pacific Rim Championships and ASEAN School Games. Historically, our competitive gymnasts have consistently won medals at club, national and international competitions.

The Club proudly hosts one of Singapore’s largest gymnastics events in the region annually – the Prime Gymnastics International Invitational Competition. Many local and international clubs and schools participate in this mega event.
